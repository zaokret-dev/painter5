Our story

Built on brushstrokes and integrity

SunState Painting was founded in 2008 by brothers Tom and Derek Lawson, who grew up painting houses with their father in East Austin. Their belief: treat every home like your own, use the best materials, and never cut corners.

Seventeen years later, we've grown to a team of 14 licensed painters — but the values haven't changed. Every crew member is background-checked, trained in-house, and held to the same high standard.

  • Licensed, bonded and fully insured in Texas
  • Written satisfaction guarantee on every job
  • Free color consultation included
  • Sherwin-Williams and Benjamin Moore certified
  • Zero-mess policy — we protect and clean up completely
  • Over 3,400 Austin homeowners served
