Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

1. Introduction

SunState Painting LLC ("we," "our," "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data when you use our services or visit our website.

3. Information we collect

  • Contact information: Name, phone number, email address, and property address
  • Project information: Details about your painting project, photos you share, service history
  • Payment information: Transaction records (we do not store credit card numbers — PayPal handles all card data securely)
  • Communications: Emails, texts, and voicemails you send us
  • Website data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, and cookie data

4. How we use your information

  • To provide services: Scheduling estimates, dispatching crews, sending invoices
  • To communicate: Appointment confirmations, follow-ups, responding to your questions
  • To improve our business: Analyzing trends, training staff, improving our website
  • For marketing: Sending promotions or tips — only with your consent, and you can opt out at any time
  • For legal compliance: Texas contractor licensing requirements, tax records, insurance documentation

5. Information sharing

We never sell your personal information. We may share data only with: PayPal Inc. (payment processing), scheduling software providers under data agreements, and law enforcement when legally required.

6. Data retention

We keep your information for as long as needed to provide service and as required by Texas law — typically 7 years for business records. You may request deletion at any time, subject to legal obligations.

7. Your rights

  • Access: Request a copy of the information we hold about you
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Ask us to delete your personal data
  • Opt-out of marketing: Unsubscribe from our emails at any time
  • California residents (CCPA): Additional rights including the right to know, delete, and opt out of data sale (we do not sell data)

To exercise any right, contact us at hello@sunstatepainting.com

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies to make our site work, and optional Google Analytics cookies to understand how visitors use the site. You can control cookies in your browser settings.

9. Data security

We use SSL encryption, secure cloud storage, and limited staff access. In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected users as required by Texas and federal law.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy periodically. We'll notify you of significant changes by posting a notice on our website or contacting you directly.

11. Contact us

Questions about this policy? Contact us at hello@sunstatepainting.com or call (512) 555-0198.
